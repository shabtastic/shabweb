How It Works

A mind, modelled

Three ideas compose the system. Together they produce something that is less background decoration and more cognitive portrait — a landscape that thinks.

01 / Semantic Graph

Concepts as nodes.
Theory as edges.

Key constructs from research publications are encoded as nodes in a weighted graph. Edges connect nodes that share theoretical relationships — the stronger the coupling, the stronger the edge. The graph is built from papers, not imposed.

02 / Spreading Activation

A mind
in motion.

An attention cursor wanders the graph — visiting nodes, boosting their activation. Energy flows outward along edges, lighting up connected concepts. The 3D surface rises wherever activation peaks, creating a landscape shaped by thought.

03 / Bayesian Surprise

Prediction error
drives change.

A global surprise parameter — semantic distance — controls how far the attention cursor jumps. Low surprise: local, predictable movement. High surprise: sudden far jumps across the graph, as if a new idea has violated prior belief. The topology doesn't just animate — it updates.

Five States of Mind

Surprise as a
narrative arc

The background cycles through five cognitive states, driven by a random walk in semantic distance. Each state has a distinct phenomenology — a different relationship between what the mind expects and what it finds. The HUD in the bottom-right corner shows which state is active.

0 – 20 % surprise

predicting

The network rests in stable equilibrium. Attention moves locally along strong edges, reinforcing familiar associations. Priors hold. The landscape barely shifts.

20 – 45 % surprise

wandering

Prediction error begins to accumulate. Attention drifts between related clusters — curiosity without commitment. The mesh ripples gently at the edges.

45 – 65 % surprise

surprised

A signal violates expectation. The pulse trails heat from blue to amber. Activation spreads beyond its local neighbourhood. Something unexpected has arrived.

65 – 82 % surprise

revising

Belief updating is underway. Distant concepts are recruited across cluster boundaries. The landscape dramatically reshapes — prior models yielding to new evidence.

82 – 100 % surprise

integrating

A new model is forming. The network seeks a new equilibrium. Understanding has expanded. The mind is more open than it was a moment ago.
