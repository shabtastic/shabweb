About

Where minds update

I am a researcher at the intersection of cognitive neuroscience, generative AI, and human flourishing. My work asks: how do minds flexibly revise their models of the world — and how can AI systems be designed to support that process?

My research focuses on psychological flexibility as a dynamic cognitive process — one that can be targeted at fine temporal resolution using neuroscientifically-grounded AI interventions. I use predictive processing and Bayesian frameworks to understand how surprise, expectancy violation, and belief updating drive creative and adaptive cognition.
